Crime Journal is an intelligent retrieval platform based on GDELT global crime data, designed to provide efficient case retrieval services for lawyers, police officers, and legal researchers. This report comprehensively analyzes the legal services market size, user needs, competitive landscape, and business opportunities through a data-driven approach.

**Key Findings:**

- **Market Size**: Global legal services market reached $1.1 trillion (2025), China market $35.1 billion, with 8.7% CAGR

- **Competitive Advantages**: AI intelligent classification, real-time updates (15-minute delay), 65 language coverage, strong price competitiveness

**Conclusion**: Crime Journal faces tremendous market opportunities and is poised to occupy an important position in the legal tech field through differentiated technological advantages and precise user positioning.
