# POLICE : Two injured in Goldsboro shooting

**Language**: English

GOLDSBORO, N.C. (WITN) - Police say a Goldsboro man and woman have been injured in a shooting.

Officials says Goldsboro Police Department officers responded to the 100 block of Vanderbilt Circle regarding two ShotSpotter alerts for a total of eight rounds.

Officers say 35-year-old Deandre Langston had been assaulted and 28-year-old Ericka Lewis showed up at UNC Health Wayne with a non-life-threatening gunshot wound to her backside.

The investigation is ongoing, according to officials. Anyone with information regarding the shooting is asked to contact Investigator Warters at 919-580-4211 or Crime Stoppers at 919-735-2255.
