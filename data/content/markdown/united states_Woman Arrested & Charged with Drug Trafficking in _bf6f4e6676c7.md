A 28-year-old woman was arrested and charged with drug trafficking after an investigation and traffic stop in Ellsworth.

Woman Taken into Custody for Aggravated Drug Trafficking

Gabrielle Battis from Lamoine was taken into custody and charged with Aggravated Drug Trafficking and Violation of Conditions of Release.

Prior Charge for Unlawful Drug Trafficking

She was out of jail on a pre-conviction bail following her arrest on June 2, 2025 for Unlawful Drug Trafficking in Fentanyl.

MDEA Conducted Surveillance of Local Business for Drug Trafficking

The Maine Drug Enforcement Agency had been doing surveillance at a local business and confirmed drug trafficking activity at the location.
