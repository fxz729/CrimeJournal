Ray Walters

Emmerdale

, and DS Walsh (Amanda Ryan) is determined to nail

Bear Wolf

(

Joshua Richards

) for the death of the latter.

While

Bear did kill Ray

, he did so after his mental health had been all but obliterated by him and his heinous mother.

Initially groomed, fed drugs and then incarcerated at a farm on which he was forced to work, Bear was swallowed by Celia’s

human trafficking and slavery racket

, and the man that emerged from the remnants of the operation bore little resemblance to the man that went in.

Bear accidentally killed Ray to protect his son,

Paddy Kirk

(

Dominic Brunt

), and

Dylan Penders

(

Fred Kettle

). Terrified that Bear would be sent to prison, imprisoned again so soon after his horrifying experience at the slave farm, Dylan convinced Paddy to move the body.

They tried to watch over Bear and ensure his silence, but a therapy session encouraged him to call the police and admit to the killing.

Paddy will do anything to protect his dad (Picture: Mark Bruce/ITV)

Despite Bear’s protestations that nobody else was involved in the crime, DS Walsh has been like a dog with a bone in trying to prove Paddy’s involvement, her discover of

the same rope

used to on Ray’s body in the vet surgery further fuelling her fire.

Fraught Paddy, desperate to contain the situation, placed Bear in

a mental health facility

after he broke his bail conditions by assaulting

Jacob Gallagher

(

Joe-Warren Plant

) in the hospital, though DS Walsh warned him that he was simply prolonging the inevitable: Bear would

eventually be released

and brought straight in for questioning…
